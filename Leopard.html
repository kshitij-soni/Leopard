My Favourite Animal-
Leopard🐆

My favorite animal is the Leopard. I know there are various species in the forest- from the majestic tigers to the always-anxious cheetals but the The secretive and nimble leopard is the creature, I am thirsty to see the most. There are 3000 tigers in India and and 13000 thousand leopard still the sighting of a leopard is far-more rare because it is a shy animal unlike a tigers which is very bold.
I have gone of many jungle safaris and have only seen a leopard once, even that wasn't a fledged sighting as I was only able to catch a glimpse.
